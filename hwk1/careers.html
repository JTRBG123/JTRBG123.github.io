Incident Response in Practice

Incident response (IR) is the structured process of preparing for, detecting, containing, and recovering from security events. Teams document procedures, practice with tabletop exercises, and review lessons learned after each incident to improve future response.

IR roles often coordinate with IT, legal, and leadership. Clear communication helps reduce downtime and ensures that evidence is handled correctly for investigations.
